English for ICT


English for Information Technology is part of the terminology taught in the courses English 2 and 3. It is offered to students of the ICSD and the Mathematics department.
The course's purpose is to provide undergraduate students with the necessary skills in order to read academic texts on Information Technology, to attend lectures and seminars and to be able to present their own work in English.
